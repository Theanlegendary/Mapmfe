KPI Pending: 3 reports staff must understand

Simple message: every pending item needs an owner, an action, and a same-day update.

1. Revenue Index

Customer created order, but money, receipt, or collection is not fully closed.

110 · 120 · 200
Daily action
  • Verify 100% outstanding receipts.
  • Update failed collection bills to Operations (PVH) daily.
  • Home collection: call customer and arrange pickup.

2. Unprocessed Inventory

Order is received, but not connected, loaded, handed over, or assigned yet.

210 · 300 · 302 · 310 / 306 · 309 · 311 · 400
Daily action
  • Connect orders by trip.
  • Assign delivery within 30 minutes after handover.
  • Check off-route orders immediately.

3. Inventory Index

Order was assigned to delivery, but delivery is still not successful.

401 · 402 · 420 · 430 · 460 · 472 · 480 · 500 · 512
Daily action
  • Update all N-1 pending status.
  • Reconfirm phone with original PO or sender.
  • After 3 failed contacts in 3 shifts, update refund status within 36h.
Trainer line: Do not just look at the number. Check why it is pending, who owns it, and what update is required today.
