💡 Include Khan / Sangkat

Adding Sangkat or Khan keywords (e.g. "សង្កាត់បឹងកេងកងទី១") guarantees 100% exact resolution over generic names.

🧹 Clean Prices & Numbers

Strip out price tags ($10, $20) or order IDs before pasting for faster Khmer fuzzy matching.

📮 1-Click PO Code Copy

Click directly on any red code in Nearby Match to copy `PNPP014` or `MEGA1` instantly to clipboard.

🌐 EN / KH Switcher

Click EN | KH in the header to switch table headers and statuses between Khmer and English.
