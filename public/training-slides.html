Pro Tip · ល្អបំផុត

Google Maps Link = Always Works

When you're not sure what to type — just copy the link from Google Maps.
នៅពេលមិនដឹងវាយអ្វី — ចម្លង link ពី Google Maps

1
Open Google Maps on your phone · បើក Google Maps នៅលើទូរស័ព្ទ
2
Find the location and tap it · រកឃើញកន្លែង ចុចលើវា
3
Tap "Share" and copy the link · ចុច "Share" ហើយចម្លង link
4
Paste into the search box · ដាក់ link ក្នុងប្រអប់ស្វែងរក

📋 Example:

https://maps.app.goo.gl/XYZ123ABC

Do's and Don'ts

✅ DO — ត្រូវវាយ

  • ផ្សារថ្មី (Khmer name)
  • Phsar Thmey (Romanized)
  • PNPP014 (Branch code)
  • maps.app.goo.gl/... (Maps link)
  • 11.5566, 104.9282 (GPS)
  • psar chas siem reap (Be specific!)

❌ DON'T — កុំវាយ

  • 2004 (years/numbers)
  • PP (too short)
  • near me (not supported)
  • 12345 (random numbers)
  • abc (meaningless)
  • psar only (too generic!)
